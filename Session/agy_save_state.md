### The Rule Context (00a §9.2)
Rule `00a §9.2` dictates that mono-resource costs (e.g., paying 2 Capital) represent a card's baseline floor-power, while **cross-resource** costs (e.g., paying 1 Capital + 1 Mandate) represent the faction stretching into higher ceiling-power actions.
