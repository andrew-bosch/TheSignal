## 3. Emergent Strategic Patterns

These emergent plays are valid under the existing SOT ruleset and require no new procedures.

### Pattern 1: Forced Contested State
1. **Setup:** A district exists where Faction A holds Dominant control (3+ chips).
2. **Action:** The acting faction acquires Faction B's native resource (via trade or Accord).
3. **Execution:** The acting faction covertly Campaigns (C03) using Faction B's resource to add chips for Faction B until their count matches Faction A's (3+ chips), forcing a Contested state.
4. **Consequence:** Battlefield Strength resolution fires at the end of the Quarter. Factions A and B fight and take losses, while the acting faction watches safely from the outside.

### Pattern 2: The Blind Weapon (Resource Trade as Setup)
1. **Setup:** The acting faction trades Capital to Faction A for Faction A's native resource during Debrief. Faction A accepts, unaware of the intent.
2. **Execution:** The acting faction uses Faction A's resource to Campaign for Faction A in a district where Faction B is Dominant.
3. **Consequence:** This forces a Contested state between Faction A and Faction B, forcing them to fight a conflict they did not plan.

### Pattern 3: Accord Fulfillment as Weapon
1. **Setup:** Faction A has an active Accord with Faction B containing a Presence Obligation: *"Faction A will achieve Established in District 7."* Faction A is currently at Present (1 chip).
2. **Execution:** The acting faction covertly Campaigns for Faction A in District 7, adding chips until Faction A reaches 3+ chips.
3. **Consequence:** Faction A's presence obligation is technically fulfilled (avoiding breach), but the district is simultaneously pushed into a Contested state with Faction B, forcing a conflict.

### Pattern 4: Accord Breach + Contested Combo
1. **Setup:** Faction A has a Territorial Prohibition Accord: *"Faction A will not exceed Established (2 chips) in District 7."* Faction A is currently at Established.
2. **Execution:** The acting faction covertly Campaigns for Faction A in District 7, placing a 3rd chip on their behalf.
3. **Consequence:** This immediately triggers an Accord breach for Faction A (dropping their Portrait score by -4 and PS by -1) while simultaneously triggering a Contested Battlefield Strength conflict.
