# Art 03 - Rules Staging Area

## Ring Resource Yields and Entry Requirements
These procedures govern how players move through the concentric rings of New Meridian and what resources those rings yield.

- **Baryo (Ring 3):** Base Resource Yield: 1 unit per Quarter. Free entry during placement.
- **The Mid (Ring 2):** Base Resource Yield: 2 units per Quarter. Restricted entry (subject to adjacency and the Ring Adjacency Penalty, M-12).
- **Core (Ring 1):** Base Resource Yield: 3 units per Quarter. Restricted entry (requires Established or Dominant presence in adjacent Ring 2 district during placement).
- **Chorus Node (Ring 0):** Base Resource Yield: None. Excluded from normal adjacency rules (placement requires Established or Dominant presence in adjacent Ring 1 district).
