**Document:** 03  
**Status:** Complete (ability costs verified against economy model)

## Design Principles

Every Operator has:
- A **passive ability** — always active, no cost
- An **active ability** — costs resources, uses the free Operative action slot
- **Three unlock tiers** — Awakening, Ascendant, Apex
- A **hidden agenda** — assigned by ARBITER from a pool, calibrated to player experience
- A **doctrine alignment map** — how each action type aligns with their beliefs

Unlock conditions are never stated to players. ARBITER delivers oblique hints at approximately 70% and 90% progress. Players receive notification when a tier is reached — never how close they are.

**Apex unlock rule:** The Apex ability must be successfully used (not just unlocked) for the operative to become a Founding Figure.

## Ability Cost Verification Notes

The following costs were revised during economy alignment:

- **Ghost Mole — Feed Disinformation:** Removed Influence requirement (now 3 Intelligence only). Ghost has minimal Influence; the action is information-based not social.
- **Warden Auditor — Expose:** Removed Intelligence requirement (now 4 Influence only). Warden exposes through institutional authority, not information gathering.
- **Warden Double Agent — Turn:** Reduced Intelligence requirement (now 4 Influence + 1 Intelligence). The Turn is primarily social.
- **Architect Contractor — Ghost Build:** Removed Intelligence requirement (now 2 Infrastructure only). Construction concealment is technique, not intelligence.

**Apex Non-Native Resource Requirements:**  
All Apex abilities require at least 1 unit of the faction's weakest resource type. This ensures the Apex decision is always a meaningful commitment and cannot be casual.
