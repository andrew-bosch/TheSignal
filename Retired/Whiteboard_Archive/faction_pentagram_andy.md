This geometric model perfectly reflects the political and philosophical tensions of The Table. Furthermore, it aligns exactly with the True State document's revelation regarding the "Apex pentagram (L165)," which confirms that the Chorus's evaluative configuration arranges the five factions at the points of a pentagram.

Here is the breakdown of the pentagonal arrangement, moving clockwise from Ghost, followed by the specific doctrinal gaps this geometry reveals:

### The Evaluative Configuration: The Faction Pentagram

**The Clockwise Sequence: Ghost → Directorate → Guild → Network → Syndicate → (Ghost)**

This arrangement ensures that every faction sits between its two closest philosophical allies (the outer pentagon edges), while staring directly across the center of the shape at its two most fundamental ideological enemies (the inner pentagram lines).

#### 1. The Alignments (Outer Pentagon Edges)

These neighbors share methods, timelines, or underlying assumptions about how power operates in New Meridian.

* **Ghost & Directorate (The Institutional Delay):** Both factions believe premature action is an existential threat. They are the factions of secrecy, containment, and institutional history, both having been present at the Chorus Node since the classified era.
* **Directorate & Guild (The Structural Authority):** Both represent the heavy, tangible reality of the city's establishment. The Directorate provides the mandate and stability, while the Guild provides the physical infrastructure and capacity.
* **Guild & Network (The Public Reality):** This is the alignment of the visible world. The Guild believes the response must exist physically in the world as a built structure. The Network believes the response must exist in the public consciousness. Neither believes the answer can be contained in a closed room.
* **Network & Syndicate (The Subversive Layer):** Both factions operate laterally, bypassing the Directorate's vertical authority. The Network utilizes pirate transmitters and grassroots exposure, while the Syndicate utilizes shell companies and gray-market capital. Both thrive when state control fails.
* **Syndicate & Ghost (The Long Game):** Both operate on massive, generational timelines rather than reacting to immediate political panic. Ghost has been embedded in the research for 31 years to understand the signal; the Syndicate was buying the ground underneath the response facilities as early as year seven. Both value structural patience.

#### 2. The Oppositions (Inner Pentagram Edges)

These are the lines that cross the center of the shape, representing fundamental doctrinal incompatibilities.

* **Ghost vs. Network (Secrecy vs. Exposure):** Ghost believes understanding must precede action and requires careful, quiet study. The Network believes secrecy is the ultimate danger and demands total transparency.
* **Ghost vs. Guild (Paralysis vs. Execution):** Ghost insists humanity does not yet understand the question and must wait. The Guild believes improvisation and delay are weaknesses, and that humanity must prove itself by physically building its answer now.
* **Directorate vs. Network (Containment vs. Disruption):** The Directorate's core belief is that survival requires control and restricted dissemination. The Network was built specifically to shatter that control and force the doors open.
* **Directorate vs. Syndicate (State Authority vs. Shadow Capital):** The Directorate seeks to maintain institutional continuity and regulatory control. The Syndicate treats state regulation as an obstacle to bypass via sovereign wealth and untraceable acquisitions.
* **Guild vs. Syndicate (Integrity vs. Commodification):** The Guild believes the physical integrity of the work *is* the response. The Syndicate does not care what the work means, only who owns the ground it stands on.

### Additions for Artifact 00.07: Doctrinal Gaps & The Shape of the Deliberation

To permanently cement this geometry into the worldbuilding, **Artifact 00.07** should be structured to explicitly highlight the systemic blind spots created by this five-point arrangement.

Design Reasoning for the Doctrinal Pentagram: 
**.07 — Doctrinal Gaps & The Geometry of The Table**
The five factions at The Table do not merely represent competing interests; they form a closed philosophical geometry. When mapped, each faction sits between two methodological allies and diametrically opposes two ideological enemies. This five-point tension creates specific, observable gaps in humanity's response.

* **The Missing "Author" Vacuum:** Look at the resources and doctrines around the pentagram. Ghost translates the question. The Guild builds the transmitter. The Syndicate funds the infrastructure. The Network broadcasts to the public. The Directorate regulates the timeline. **The Gap:** *None of the five factions are doctrinally equipped to write the actual message.* Every faction is fighting over the medium, the timing, the ownership, or the visibility of the response, but none of them are designated to generate the *content* of the response. (True state: The Chorus is measuring the pattern of the factions fighting over the microphone because humanity has no idea what to actually say into it.)
* **The Shared Edge of Anti-Obfuscation:** The most fragile alignment at The Table is the operational overlap between the Guild and the Network. While the Guild loves order and the Network loves disruption, they share a fundamental hatred of the Directorate's classified red tape. The Guild hates it because it halts construction; the Network hates it because it hides the truth. This shared edge represents the "tangible reality" vector—the factions that force The Table's abstract ideas into the physical streets of New Meridian.
* **The Inevitability of Doctrinal Betrayal:** Because of this pentagonal structure, any binary question brought to The Table will inherently split the factions 3 to 2. This mathematical reality guarantees that to secure a majority, at least one faction must side with an ideological enemy to defeat another. (True State: The Table is designed such that political victory often requires philosophical betrayal—a dissonance the Chorus is actively evaluating.)


Changes proposed for 00.07:
To fully lock this geometric arrangement into place, the foundational faction doctrines are already about 90% aligned with this pentagram.

However, to make the edges of the shape mathematically perfect and to support the new ** Doctrinal Gaps** section above, we should make a few surgical adjustments to the core faction descriptions.

Here is what needs to be added or changed in the faction doctrine statements in Art 00.07 to reinforce the arrangement:

### 1. Fortify the Guild-Network Edge (The Anti-Obfuscation Alignment)

This is the most fragile connection on the board. The Network wants public transparency ("Opacity is not stability. It's pressure."), while the Guild wants physical execution ("Whatever we send... will exist in the world. As structure."). To make them true neighbors, the Guild's doctrine needs to explicitly acknowledge that building at scale inherently destroys the Directorate's containment.

* **Change to The Guild's Doctrine:** Add a statement emphasizing that infrastructure cannot be classified.
* **Suggested Addition:** *“The Guild knows that planetary-scale infrastructure cannot be hidden behind Directorate redactions. To build the response is to expose it. They share the Network’s disdain for closed-door secrecy, not out of a belief in public rights, but because you cannot pour three thousand cubic meters of reinforced aggregate in the dark.”*

### 2. Fortify the Syndicate-Ghost Edge (The Generational Alignment)

Ghost and the Syndicate sit next to each other because they are the only two factions immune to the short-term political panic of The Table. Ghost has been studying the signal for 31 years; the Syndicate has been buying the ground underneath the response facilities since year seven.

* **Change to The Syndicate's Doctrine:** Their current doctrine emphasizes control and infrastructure ("whoever controls the response infrastructure... controls the response"). It needs to explicitly claim their timeline to mirror Ghost's patience.
* **Suggested Addition:** *“The Syndicate does not operate on the frantic timeline of political panic. Like Ghost, they play a generational long game. While the Directorate and Network fight over what happens tomorrow, the Syndicate possesses the structural patience to secure the land, wait for the factions to realize what it is for, and lease it back to them.”*

### 3. Sharpen the Directorate's Oppositions (The Axis of Control)

The Directorate currently sits exactly opposite the Network (Disruption) and the Syndicate (Shadow Capital). Their doctrine ("Survival requires control, restraint, and continuity") is solid, but it needs to explicitly state why they view the Syndicate's financial lateral moves as just as dangerous as the Network's pirate broadcasts.

* **Change to The Directorate's Doctrine:** Clarify that "uncoordinated action" applies specifically to rogue capital, not just rogue information.
* **Suggested Addition:** *“The Directorate views the Network’s grassroots leaks and the Syndicate’s gray-market acquisitions as two sides of the same existential threat: the bypassing of state authority. Survival requires that both the narrative and the infrastructure remain under vertical, institutional control.”*

### 4. Preserve the "Missing Author" Vacuum

Look closely at the opening statements provided for each faction:

* **Ghost:** Asks if we understand the question.
* **Network:** Demands the public be in the room.
* **Syndicate:** Demands ownership of the transmission platform.
* **Guild:** Demands the right to build the transmitter.
* **Directorate:** Demands control over the timeline.

**The Overarching Design Rule:** We must strictly ensure that as we continue to develop card flavor text and Chronicle entries, **no faction ever claims to know exactly what the message should be.** The geometry only works if every faction is fighting over the *medium*, the *timing*, or the *ownership* of the response. If any faction produces a draft of the actual message, the geometric tension collapses. The "Missing Author" doctrinal gap must remain a deliberate, glaring omission in the worldbuilding.
